About Us

We have been actively in the software industry since 2011. We have been continuing our adventure that we started by providing business - process managements, mobile applications and consultancy services, since we met the devices called Beacon in 2014 and we have been developing extensive projects. The indoor navigation application that we realized in 2017 in Fair İzmir using Beacon devices has been a stimulating experience for our projects that we are currently developing. We were one of the founding companies of Adnan Menderes University Technopark in 2018. Today, we are developing comprehensive and multi-disciplinary projects such as "Smart Agriculture, Digital Campus, Digital Tourism".

Leverage Bilişim is an ADÜ Technopark company that has always adopted the principle of providing the highest quality solutions to its customers with its expert team and academic consultants.
