Data Mining

Data mining is the business of accessing useful information from large-scale data and searching for information. It can also be defined as the search for the relations that can enable us to make predictions about the future from large data stacks using a computer program. As Leverage Bilişim, we offer data mining and machine learning integration options to our customers in our big data oriented projects. Data-oriented, learning systems in each module with process controls and decision support mechanisms can be a solution to our customers' problems. Creating scalable and manageable systems is a must for solutions to be long-term and developable. For this reason, we prefer to produce machine learning modules as solutions for a smaller part that will support the solution of the general problem as micro services.

Leverage Bilişim is an ADÜ Technopark company that has always adopted the principle of providing the highest quality solutions to its customers with its expert team and academic consultants.
